Privacy Policy

JobberMed values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to the newsletter, we collect your email address. We may also collect limited analytics data (such as page visits) to improve the website experience.

How We Use Information

We use your email address only to send job updates and relevant announcements. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing.

Data Storage

Newsletter subscriptions are managed through Brevo (Sendinblue). Your data is stored securely under their platform and subject to their privacy and security practices.

Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email. If you need help, contact us through the website.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
